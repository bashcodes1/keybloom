Our Services

Our services at Keybloom are designed to help emerging artists reach their full potential and achieve success in the music industry. From publishing and distribution to public relations and global royalty collection, we provide a range of support and resources to help our artists succeed. Additionally, we offer educational sync licensing webinars to help artists understand the industry and get their music placed in film, TV, and other media. We are dedicated to finding new and innovative ways to support and promote the talents of our artists.

SYNC LICENSING WEBINARS

Global Royalty Collection

LET'S HELP YOU COLLECT ALL YOUR ROYALTIES

Our team at Keybloom is dedicated to helping artists navigate the complex process of global royalty collection. We understand the importance of tracking and collecting royalties for your music, and we are here to make sure you are fairly compensated for your hard work. We can help you identify potential royalty streams, set up systems for tracking and collecting royalties, and negotiate deals with collection societies and other organizations. Let us take the stress and confusion out of royalty collection so you can focus on what matters most - creating music.
