About us

Keybloom focuses on showcasing the talents of emerging artists and helping them reach a wider audience through the promotion of their work. We specialize in helping artists secure publishing deals and navigate the music industry.

Our Services

MUSIC PUBLISHING

Music publishing is the process of managing and distributing the intellectual property rights of a song or composition. It involves securing publishing deals, collecting royalties, and protecting the rights of the artist.

MUSIC DISTRIBUTION (AUDIO & VIDEO)

Music distribution is the process of getting music and video content to reach a wider audience through various channels and platforms. We specialize in audio and video distribution for emerging artists.

SYNC LICENSING WEBINARS

Sync licensing webinars are educational sessions that teach artists and industry professionals about the process of securing sync licenses for music in film, TV, and other media. We offer a variety of sync licensing webinars to help artists succeed in the industry.

GLOBAL ROYALTY COLLECTION

Global royalty collection in the music industry involves tracking and collecting royalties for artists from the use of their music in various countries around the world. We specialize in helping artists navigate the complex process of collecting these royalties.
